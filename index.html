Mission Statement

Our vision is to help give inmates access to education and build study skills to create productive members of society and reduce the chance of first-time offenders from reoffending while providing support to our teachers.

Values

We believe:

all students have a right to a fair and equitable education, whether they have committed a crime or not;

all students deserve to form their own opinion;

all students deserve a bright and fulfilling future;

all students deserve to be treated with respect, whether from student to student or from teacher to student;

all office members must uphold their responsibilities to achieve a quality education experience;

all office members must provide equal treatment under the law; and

in supporting our free-lance teachers.
